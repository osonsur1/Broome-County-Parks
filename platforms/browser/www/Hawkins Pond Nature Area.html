Hawkins Pond Nature Area


Hawkins Pond Nature Area offers a secluded "nature experience" with its plentiful wildlife and wide variety of habitat-types. Approximately 4 miles of nature trails lead visitors through woods and fields and around the scenic 24-acre pond. Hawkins Pond Nature Area is a participant in the Wegman's "Passport to Family Wellness" program.

Online reservations for picnic shelters: reserveamerica.com

For more information, call 607.778.2193 or 607.693.1389.
