Broome County Parks Triathlon/Duathlon/AquaBike, June 30, 2018 - Dorchester Park, Whitney Point

This annual event brings together athletes (adults and youth) and spectators at beautiful Dorchester Park! The 9:00am Triathlon/Duathlon/AquaBike events are followed by the untimed Youth Triathlon, for ages 7- 13. Sign-up information and more details can be found at RunSignUp.com, or call us at (607)778-2193.

Free admission for spectators! Come for the event, then stay for swimming, boating, and picnicking.

A portion of the event's proceeds will support the Broome County Parks Handicapped Children's Playground Fund, to increase play opportunities for all children at Broome County Parks.

Many thanks to our 2018 sponsors: Coughlin & Gerhart, Chenango Point Cycles, ERA Decker Real Estate, Tom's Coffee, Cards & Gifts, and the Broome County Triathlon Club.

2018 "Broome Bands Together" Free Summer Concert Series - Otsiningo Park - 6:00PM - 8:00PM

New in 2018: The festivities begin with an opening act at 6:00 PM, followed by the headliner band at 6:30pm. Admission and parking is free! Enjoy a variety of music styles performed by popular local favorites as well as some out-of-town surprises! Bring lawn chairs or a blanket to these family-friendly, alcohol-free and tobacco-free events, sponsored by Coughlin & Gerhart, LLP. Pack a picnic dinner,or purchase food at the park. For more information call (607)778-2193, or check out the events listings on facebook.



2018 Spiedie Fest and Balloon Rally at Otsiningo Park - Aug 3, 4, 5, 2018

Spiedies, hot air balloons, spiedies, live music, spiedies, fireworks, spiedies, crafts, and so much more! This 3-day family-friendly event is not to be missed! Visit www.SpiedieFest.com for more information.
