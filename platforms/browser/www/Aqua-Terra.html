Aqua-Terra Wilderness Area


Formerly the site of the Aqua-Terra Sportsman's Club and Innsbruck USA ski area, Aqua-Terra Wilderness Area offers 466 acres of largely undeveloped natural settings. A 6-acre pond provides a secluded fishing experience* and opportunities for wildlife-watching.

Find us on facebook!

As at all Broome County Parks' facilities, admission is free. Visitors are required to be aware of and follow Parks' rules:

  • Dogs are permitted but must be kept on a leash no longer than 8-ft in length; owners must pick up after their dogs.
  • Motorized vehicles are not permitted on the trails.
  • Camping is not permitted.
  • Fishing from shore is permitted; swimming, wading, and boats are not permitted.

Aqua-Terra Wilderness Area is located off Maxian Road in the Town of Binghamton.
