Grippen Park


Grippen Park, located on Grippen Ave, Endicott, offers a new dimension to year-round recreation in Broome County.

Enjoy a simple picnic lunch, or plan a big family event. The "float-off" boat ramp offers easy access to the Susquehanna River.

Online reservations for picnic shelters: reserveamerica.com

Find us on facebook!

  • Shelter - 100 person capacity, available by reservation online - call 607.778.2193. Shelter rental rates are $90.00 on weekends/holidays, with a reduced rate of $30.00 on non-holiday weekdays.
  • Baseball field
  • Children's play structure
  • Boat launch
  • Tables and grills
  • Water taps and fountain
  • Horseshoe pits

Indoor BMX at Grippen Park is operated by an independent organization; for information, click here: grippenparkbmx.com

Take Rt. 17 East to Exit 66. Turn right at end of ramp and go .2 miles to right turn "To Rt. 17C". Cross over bridge to traffic signal at Rt.17C. FROM THE EAST:
Take Rt. 17 West to Exit 67 North. Crossover bridge and take exit ramp to Rt. 17C West (Main Street). Follow 17C west to the 8thtraffic signal (2.0 miles) to South Grippen Avenue. Turn left onto South Grippen Avenue and go to the end of the street.

FROM THE NORTH OR SOUTH:
Take I-81 North or South to Rt. 17 West. Then follow directions from the east (above).

FROM THE NORTHEAST:
Take I-88 East to I-81 South to Rt. 17 West. Then follow directions from the east (above).
