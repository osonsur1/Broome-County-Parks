Greenwood Park


Greenwood is the County's first and most diverse park. The well-maintained campground offers the entire family a quality camping experience. The lake's spring-fed waters provide refreshing swimming areas at the white sand beach, as well as great opportunities for anglers of all ages, from shore or from a rental boat. Relax in the shade or bask in the sun in the extensive picnic areas, or enjoy the peaceful beauty of the park's nature trail system. In winter, this year-round park offers ice-fishing, and miles of groomed cross-country ski trails and equipment rentals. Organized youth groups may secure a permit to use the Youth Group Camping Area, located on the northwest side of the lake.

Cross-Country Skiing

*Availability of skiing is dependent upon snow and trail conditions. Check for announcements on facebook, or call ahead: 607.862.9933 or 607.778.2193.

Almost 6 miles of groomed trails provide opportunities for beginners through expert skiers. Snowshoes or skis are required on the trails (no hiking). Bring your own equipment and pay only a $5.00 trail use fee for the day, or rent equipment at the park (no trail use fee for renters.) Season passes are available at the park. Children's sizes are available, beginning with approximately age 4.
Hours of equipment rental operation:

  • 8:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m. weekdays
  • 9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m. weekends

Equipment rental rates:

  • Cross-Country Skis or Snowshoes - $5.00 per hour
  • No additional Trail Use Fee if renting equipment.

Trail use fee (Non-Renters):

  • $5.00 per day, or Season Pass for $35.00

All trails begin at the ski concession stand and rental operation. Please, no dogs or other pets on the ski trails at any time.
