Roundtop Picnic Area


Roundtop Picnic Area sits high above Endicott, offering spectacular views looking to the east and west through the Susquehanna River Valley. This picnic area became a reality through the combined efforts of Broome County, the Village of Endicott and the Endicott Rotary Club. It is a haven for families and individuals seeking a pleasant and relaxing day away from work.

For more information, call 607-778-2193 or 607-778-6541.

Online reservations for picnic shelters: reserveamerica.com

For more information, call 607.778.2193 or 607.693.1389.

FROM THE WEST:
Take Rt. 17 East to Exit 66. Turn right at end of ramp and go .2 miles to right turn "To Rt. 17C". Cross over bridge to traffic signal at Rt.17C. Turn right onto 17C East and travel 4.6 miles to Lillian Avenue. Turn right on Lillian Avenue and go to end and bear right up hill to Roundtop Picnic Area entrance.

FROM THE EAST:
Take Rt. 17 West to Exit 67 North. Crossover bridge and take exit ramp to Rt. 17C West (Main Street). Follow 17C west 1.6 miles to Lillian Avenue. Turn left onto Lillian Avenue. Go to end and bear right uphill to Roundtop Picnic Area entrance.

FROM THE NORTH OR SOUTH:
Take I-81 North or South to Rt. 17 West. Then follow directions from the east (above).

FROM THE NORTHEAST:
Take I-88 East to I-81 South to Rt. 17 West. Then follow directions from the east above.
