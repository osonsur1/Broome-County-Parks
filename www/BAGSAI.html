BAGSAI Softball Complex


Binghamton Area Girls Softball Association Incorporated.

This association is made up of volunteers who promote competitive girls and women's softball in the Broome County area. Through a cooperative effort by this association and the Broome County Department of Parks and Recreation, a championship four-field softball complex has evolved, home to both local and national tournaments.

For more information, call 607.761.8095

  • Four softball fields
  • Parking
  • Restrooms
  • Wheelchair accessibility
  • Drinking fountains, water
  • Food Concession
